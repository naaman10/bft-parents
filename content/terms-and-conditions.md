
# Terms and Conditions

**Brighter Futures Tutoring**

### 1. Fees
1. Tuition fees are charged at £32 per session.
2.  Invoices will be issued on the 23rd of each month.
3. All invoices must be paid in full by the 1st of the following month.
4. Failure to make payment by the due date may result in suspension of lessons until payment is received.

### 2. Payment Terms
1. Payment must be made via the payment method stated on the invoice.
2. It is the responsibility of the parent/guardian to ensure payment is made on time.
3. The tutor reserves the right to withdraw a student’s place if fees remain unpaid.

### 3. Late Payment
1. If payment is not received by the 1st of the month, tuition sessions may be suspended until full payment is received.
2. Continued non-payment may result in permanent termination of tuition services.
3. The tutor reserves the right to recover any outstanding fees through appropriate means if necessary.

### 4. Cancellation and Refund Policy
1. If tuition is cancelled by the parent/guardian during the course of a month, fees already invoiced or paid are non-refundable.
2. Notice of cancellation must be provided in writing.
3. No refunds will be issued for missed sessions unless otherwise agreed at the tutor’s discretion.

### 5. Rescheduling and Tutor Availability
1. The tutor reserves the right to reschedule lessons due to holidays, illness, or other reasonable circumstances.
2. Rescheduled lessons will be arranged at a mutually agreed date and time suitable for both the tutor and the parent/guardian.
3. Reasonable notice will be provided wherever possible.

### 6. Safeguarding
1. The safety and wellbeing of all students is a priority.
2. All sessions will be conducted in a professional manner and in an appropriate learning environment.
3. For in-person tuition, a parent/guardian must ensure that a responsible adult is present at the property for the duration of the session (unless otherwise agreed).
4. For online tuition, sessions should take place in an appropriate space within the home.
5. Any safeguarding concerns will be handled in line with UK safeguarding guidance and, where necessary, may be reported to the appropriate authorities.

### 7. Data Protection (UK GDPR)
1. The tutor will collect and process personal data (such as names, contact details, and relevant educational information) solely for the purpose of providing tuition services.
2. Personal data will be stored securely and will not be shared with third parties unless required by law or with parental consent.
3. Parents/guardians have the right to request access to the personal data held about them or their child and to request correction of inaccurate information.
4. Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil contractual and legal obligations.

### 8. General
1. By enrolling in tuition sessions, the parent/guardian agrees to these Terms and Conditions.
2. These Terms and Conditions may be updated from time to time, and the latest version will be provided where changes occur.
3. The tutor reserves the right to terminate tuition with reasonable notice if there is a breach of these Terms and Conditions.
4. Brighter Futures Tutoring has a zero policy tolerance to abusive or threatening behaviour from pupils or parents and reserves the right to terminate tuition.
